Decentralized Flow

dFlow demonstrates how to simplify bureaucracy by using blockchain to streamline the process of acquiring and using credentials.

Let's get started!

This new service uses cutting-edge blockchain-based technology to issue credentials, while ensuring all pre-requisites are met.

  1. Select the credential you wish to check/obtain from the drop-down list.
  2. Find your legal entity. Don't worry, this step is optional and you will be able to create it if you don't already have one.
  3. Click the begin button!
